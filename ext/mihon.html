Mihon & Aniyomi ​

Due to DMCA takedown notices, Tachiyomi (now continued as Mihon) and Aniyomi no longer come with preloaded extensions. You have to sideload extension repos in Mihon, Aniyomi, Dantotsu and their forks to get extensions.

Mihon & forks ​

Adding Repo ​

  • Auto: Click on Install and the repo will be sideloaded automatically.
  • Manual: Copy the URL (from the manual column) and put it in More -> Settings -> Browse -> Extension repos.

Adding Extension ​

  1. After adding the repo, go to Home -> Browse -> Anime/Manga Extension.
  2. Download & install your desired extension by clicking .
  3. Find it in the "Installed" section (at the top of the list) with beside it.
  4. The extension is disabled by default, so click on the icon to enable it.
